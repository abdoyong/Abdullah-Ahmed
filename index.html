Specialized Academic Research

Assessing the Impact of Saher System on Driver Behavior at Signalized Intersections in Dammam

Graduation Project - Traffic and Transportation Engineering

My graduation project focused on evaluating the impact of red light camera enforcement (Saher system) on driver behavior at signalized intersections in Dammam, Saudi Arabia. The research specifically analyzed two important traffic engineering metrics:

Start-up Lost Time

The time lost at the beginning of the green signal

Clearance Lost Time

The time lost at the end of the green signal

Methodology:

Field Data Collection
Traffic Simulation Tools
Advanced Statistical Analysis

Study Location

Dammam, Saudi Arabia

Results & Recommendations:

The study provided valuable insights into how automated enforcement impacts intersection performance and overall traffic efficiency. The findings support future policies aimed at enhancing road safety and improving traffic operations in urban areas.
